Magnify!

Created by Grade 12 ICT Students (CP12A1): Ron Asnahon, Carl John Arpay, Shelina Mae Dela Cruz, Sean Ricaza, Joeron Zeniff Paloma, Van Erich Nisnisan, Vince Gerard Gonzaga and James Virtucio as part of their Entrepreneurship Business Plan.

Empowering Financial Literacy

Financial literacy is essential, yet it's not always prioritized in formal education. Magnify! aims to help users gain confidence in managing money, understanding budgeting, and making informed financial decisions.

Simplified Financial Tracking

Consolidating essential finance management tools in one place, from tracking transactions to visualizing spending patterns, helping users make better financial decisions.

Responsible Spending Habits

Promoting better money management through budget planning, spending analysis, and personalized suggestions to build habits that positively impact future financial health.

Customizable Financial Tools

Supporting personal financial goals with tailored tools like budgeting, mortgage calculators, and tax estimations that align with each user's unique situation.

Convenient Bill Payments

Integrated secure bills payment portal with receipt-style confirmation enhances transparency and helps maintain good financial standing.

Educational Resources

Curated resources for deepening knowledge of budgeting, financial planning, and responsible spending, emphasizing financial education as a continuous journey.

Digital Age Finance

Engaging and visually appealing platform with real-time updates, interactive UI elements, and consistent access to financial data from any device.

Student-Focused Solutions

Tailored for University of Cebu Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue students, addressing the gap in personal finance education for young adults.

Building Financial Community

Cultivating a community of informed, financially independent users who are proactive in managing their finances for long-term benefits.
